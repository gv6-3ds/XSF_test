Modelica.Electrical.Machines.Thermal.SynchronousMachines.ThermalAmbientSMPM

Thermal ambient for synchronous machine with permanent magnets

Information

Thermal ambient for synchronous machines with permanent magnets to prescribe winding temperatures either constant or via signal connectors. Additionally, all losses = heat flows are recorded.

Extends from Machines.Interfaces.InductionMachines.PartialThermalAmbientInductionMachines (Partial thermal ambience for induction machines).
